Meet WH R. Lane

WH R. Lane is a husband, father, and U.S. military veteran running for Congress to stand up for working families and the communities that raised him. He believes opportunity should never be determined by zip code—and that leadership begins with service. Raised in Southern California by his parents, Rita and Anthony Foster, Lane worked as a security manager at the Long Beach Airport before enlisting in the United States Army. As an infantryman, he earned multiple medals and awards, completed several non-combat deployments, and holds a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science—bringing together discipline, leadership, and modern problem-solving. Lane and his wife—his partner of 13 years and wife of 9—were both raised in the inner cities of Los Angeles, specifically Compton and Long Beach. Together, they are raising their two children, Noah (9) and Peyton (8), who are the center of their world. As a family, they enjoy hiking, exploring the outdoors, and trying new foods.
